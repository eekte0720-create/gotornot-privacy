GotorNot - Account Deletion Request

Last updated: June 11, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your GotorNot account and all associated data by following the steps below.

Option 1: In-App Deletion
  1. Open the GotorNot app
  2. Go to My Page
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion
Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to gotornot.official@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address.

We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • All posts and uploaded content
  • Wish, Got, and Skip lists
  • Followers and following lists
  • Comments and votes

Data Retention

Please Note: Some data may be retained for up to 30 days after deletion for backup and legal compliance purposes. After this period, all data will be permanently deleted.
